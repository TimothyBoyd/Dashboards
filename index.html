Mapping Homelessness and Supporting Resources in San Diego

Interactive Point-In-Time Count (PITC) Density & Resource Dashboards

https://sdheart.sdsu.edu/

This project is developed by the Center for Human Dynamics in the Mobile Age (HDMA) at San Diego State University. HDMA is a transdisciplinary research center focused on understanding dynamic patterns in human behavior, mobility, and communication. This work is supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF) and contributes to data-driven approaches for addressing homelessness and improving access to critical community resources.
